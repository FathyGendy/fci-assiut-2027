# FCI Assiut · Class of 2027

A modern, immersive, and visually stunning digital yearbook for the Faculty of Computers and Information (FCI) at Assiut University and Assiut National University, Class of 2027. 

This interactive website serves as a living archive for classmates to celebrate their journey, explore profiles, share memories, and lock thoughts away in a digital time capsule.

---

## Features

- **Cosmic Dark & Light Modes:** Seamless transition between a rich, ambient dark theme and a clean, high-contrast light theme.
- **Interactive Classmate Directory:**
  - View beautiful senior profiles with major, city, and custom captions.
  - **The Freshman Flip:** Hover or click cards to flip between Senior portraits and classic Freshman photos to see how everyone has grown!
  - Advanced search and responsive filtering by University and City tabs.
  - Interactive "Add Yourself" wizard to dynamically insert new classmates with profile photos.
- **Senior Memory Wall:** A collaborative wall of digital polaroids. Classmates can post memories, choose stylized signature typography, and upload photos.
- **Digital Time Capsule:** A futuristic, countdown-locked capsule. Messages written to the future are locked under a secure countdown timer until the official Gala event on **June 23, 2027**.
- **Fully Responsive & Accessible:** Designed to provide a flawless, high-contrast, tactile experience across mobile devices, tablets, and wide screens.

---

## Tech Stack

- **Framework:** [Angular (v21+)](https://angular.dev/)
  - Designed fully **Zoneless** for ultra-fast, modern reactive performance.
  - Managed state using Angular **Signals** and `computed()` selectors.
  - Modern reactive forms with built-in validation.
- **Styling:** [Tailwind CSS v4+](https://tailwindcss.com/)
  - Custom fluid display typography ("Space Grotesk" & "JetBrains Mono").
  - Seamless 3D card flips, perspective animations, and custom vector icons.
- **Backend/Storage:** [Firebase Cloud Firestore](https://firebase.google.com/) for real-time, durable storage of classmate profiles, memories, and time capsule messages.
